# Boids → space theme

**App:** `apps/boids`

## Idea

Reskin the boids simulation with a space theme:

- Replace each **boid** with a **spacerocket** sprite.
- Replace the **cursor** (the thing the flock steers toward / away from) with a
  **spaceman**.

## Notes

- Behaviour (flocking rules) stays the same — this is a visual reskin only.
- Rocket sprites should point in their direction of travel.
