# Inksplat — blot-drawing app

**App:** `apps/inksplat` (new)

## Idea

A digital take on the *Inksplat* art form: the screen shows random blots/dots,
and the user draws lines and doodles on top to turn each blot into a picture —
whatever they imagine (a face, an animal, a scene).

## Sketch of behaviour

- Generate a canvas of random blots (size, colour, placement varied).
- User draws freehand lines over the blots to complete a drawing.
- "New blots" regenerates the layout; drawing clears.

## Nice-to-haves (later)

- Adjustable blot density / palette.
- Undo / clear.
- Export the finished drawing as an image.
